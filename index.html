Napoleonic Wars Simulator

Rules

Recruit soldiers with gold to build your French army to glory!
Each move, you can declare war on other teams. The next term, you can attack them, and your chances of winning the battle are based on your army size and morale. More wins, higher morale.
But beware! They can declare war on you, too! Check your statistics to ensure you don't make any mistakes.
